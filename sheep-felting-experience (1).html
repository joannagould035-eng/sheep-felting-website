About Your Host

I'm Joanna, a sheep farmer and felting enthusiast who runs Meadow House, a sustainable smallholding in the beautiful Welsh countryside.

Here at Meadow House, we keep sheep in an organic, sustainable environment alongside our ponies, bees, hens, cats, and dogs. I love sharing the traditional craft of felting using wool from our own sheep, and there's nothing better than seeing guests connect with our animals and learn a new skill.
